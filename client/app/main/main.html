Jenkins Shepherd

Nokia: Innovative Projects Winter 2014.

Welcome to Jenkins Shepherd - facilitation of your Jenkins servers:

  • Web application to manage multiple jenkins applications.
  • Check your built status of your jobs
  • Display list of installed plugins
  • Migrate one jenkins to another server (Copy all jobs and all plugins)
  • Queue jobs in any order via scheduler
  • Full control of jobs on your servers:
    • Enable, disable and delete single job/multiply jobs
    • Rerun your builds and check their last success
    • Show your XML config of job
    • Copy your builds on other servers
  • Yeoman application starring:
    • AngularJS on frontend
    • ExpressJS on backend
    • MongoDb as database
    • Bootstrap in UI
    • Socket.io as bond between db and app
